About Live Rock

Live Rock is a blog that cover all of your rock and metal music news. We will keep you up to date with upcoming gigs, recent gig reviews, new bands and more.

Right now there are two of us, Astrid and Matt, that are working around the clock to keep you all up to date with the latest news. I, Astrid, focus on the recent and upcoming gigs and Matt takes on all the other news that you want to hear about.

We like to think that we do a good job in covering everything that you guys want to know and read, but if you think there is anything that we have missed or something you want to read about then head over to the contact page and drop us a message and we will see what we can do!
